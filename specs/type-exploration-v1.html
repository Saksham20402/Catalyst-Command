Direction C / 04
Syne

The designer's grotesque. Syne has an architectural eccentricity — the g, a, and t are slightly constructed, giving it a presence at display sizes that most grotesques lack. At body size it reads clean. At 72px it reads like a brand statement.

Direction D / 04
Fraunces
+ Urbanist

The unexpected warmth play. Fraunces is a variable "wonky" serif — it has an optical-size axis and a "wonk" axis that gives display text a slightly playful, artisan quality. Paired with Urbanist (a geometric humanist sans) the system reads as warm, trustworthy, and genuinely different from every other edtech product.

Warm authority Serif display Humanist body Most distinctive

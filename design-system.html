Foundations

Colors

Five tokens make up the entire Catalyst palette. Each has a specific role — resist the urge to reach for outside colors.

Core Palette

Background
#1F1F1F
Page background, deepest layer. Never use on elements.
Card
#2F2F2F
Bento cards, panels, any raised surface.
Catalyst Green
#46675D
Brand color. Primary actions, links, active states. Our identity.
Mist
#BEDCE4
Heatmaps, info states, secondary highlights. High frequency.
Gold
#D3C182
Accent only. Warning states, "review" status, rare highlights.

Semantic Colors

Success BG
Green · 25% opacity
Done status, positive states.
Warning BG
Gold · 15% opacity
Review status, caution states.
Danger BG
#DC5050 · 15% opacity
Critical priority, errors, destructive actions.
Border
White · 7% opacity
Dividers, card borders, subtle separators.

Text Colors

#EAE9E5 — Primary text
Headings, body copy, active labels. Warm off-white — chosen over #FFFFFF because the canvas (#1F1F1F) has a warm undertone; pure white reads cold and clinical against it. EAE9E5 harmonises with the palette.
#787878 — Muted text
Metadata, placeholders, secondary labels
#FFFFFF — High-emphasis only
Use sparingly — button labels on CatalystGreen CTAs, or single-word emphasis where maximum contrast is required. Never for body copy or headings.
